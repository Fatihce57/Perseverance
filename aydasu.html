NASA recently announced that - for the first time - we have confirmed the water molecule, H2O, in sunlit areas of the Moon. This indicates that water is widely distributed across the lunar surface. During this same time period, using ultra high precision laboratory equipment, scientists also found water molecules locked in glasses and minerals in the samples returned by the Apollo missions.
